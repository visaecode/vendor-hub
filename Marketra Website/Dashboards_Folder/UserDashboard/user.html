Registration Guide

How to apply for a market stall at Oaktown City Public Market.

Prepare these before you start

  • Valid Government ID (PhilSys, Driver's License, SSS, or Passport)
  • DTI or SEC Business Registration Certificate
  • DOH Health Certificate
  • TIN Number (optional but recommended)
Step 1

Create Your Account

Fill in your personal details — name, contact info, address, and a valid government ID.

Step 2

Enter Business Information

Provide your business name, type, product category, and TIN number if available.

Step 3

Select Stall Preferences

Choose your preferred zone, stall size, and operating days and hours.

Step 4

Upload Documents

Upload your valid ID, DTI/SEC registration, and DOH health certificate.

Step 5

Review & Submit

Double-check everything, agree to the declaration, and submit your application.

Step 6

Pay the Permit Fee

After approval, pay the computed fee via Cash, GCash, Maya, or Bank Transfer.

Step 7

Receive Your Permit

Pick up your permit at the Market Admin Office in Zone A. Bring your ID and receipt.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about market stall registration and operations.

REGISTRATION

Who can apply for a stall?

Any Filipino citizen aged 18 and above with a legitimate business. You'll need a valid ID, DTI/SEC registration, and a DOH health certificate.

How long does processing take?

Standard processing tracks between 5 to 7 business days from complete file clearance submission details.

What if my application is rejected?

You will receive a notification highlighting the gaps. You can rectify documents and apply again immediately.

PERMITS & RENEWAL

When should I renew?

Permit renewal must be filled out prior to the designated deadline to maintain compliance flags safely.

What documents do I need for renewal?

You need an updated Health Certificate, Barangay Clearance, and a Valid Government ID.

What happens if I miss the deadline?

Late filings incur incremental surcharges, and unrenewed slots risk immediate reassignment access pools.

PAYMENTS & FEES

How are fees calculated?

Fees are calculated relative to total target floor area sizing and structural zones categories metrics mapping.

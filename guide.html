Registration Guide

How to apply for a market stall at Oaktown City Public Market.

Prepare these before you start

  • Valid Government ID (PhilSys, Drivers License, SSS, or Passport)
  • DTI or SEC Business Registration Certificate
  • DOH Health Certificate (current year, 2026)
  • TIN Number (optional but recommended)
Step 1

Create Your Account

Fill in your personal details - name, contact info, address, and a valid government ID.

Step 2

Enter Business Information

Provide your business name, type, product category, and TIN number if available.

Step 3

Select Stall Preferences

Choose your preferred zone, stall size, and operating days and hours.

Step 4

Upload Documents

Upload your valid ID, DTI/SEC registration, and DOH health certificate (current year).

Step 5

Review and Submit

Double-check everything, agree to the declaration, and submit your application.

Step 6

Pay the Permit Fee

After approval, pay the computed fee via Cash, GCash, Maya, or Bank Transfer.

Step 7

Receive Your Permit

Pick up your permit at the Market Admin Office in Zone A. Bring your ID and receipt.

Processing Timeline

Document Verification 1-3 days
Admin Review 3-5 days
Stall Assignment Day 5
Permit Issuance After payment
